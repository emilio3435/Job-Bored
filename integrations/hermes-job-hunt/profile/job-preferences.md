## Scoring rubric

Use a 1–10 fit score. Prefer writing to Pipeline only when fit score is 8+ unless Emilio changes the gate.

Weighted dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | What earns full credit |
|---|---:|---|
| Consulting / strategy / AI-tooling lane fit | 18 | Role is digital marketing consultant, strategist, AI builder, AI tooling, or applied AI strategy. |
| AI / LLM relevance | 15 | Company or role involves LLMs, agents, RAG, AI workflows, AI search, or applied GenAI. |
| Performance marketing / unit economics | 12 | Paid acquisition, CAC/LTV/ROAS, bidding, attribution, channel forecasting, growth. |
| Adtech / martech / media relevance | 8 | Marketing technology, media, adtech, measurement, CTV/OTT, programmatic, CDP, retail media, audio. |
| Seniority fit | 10 | Senior manager, principal IC, staff, director, head-of, or strategic lead level. |
| Compensation transparency | 8 | Salary or OTE band published. Missing salary penalizes but does not auto-reject. |
| Remote / location fit | 12 | Remote or hybrid in Denver, Philadelphia, or Little Rock. |
| Company credibility | 9 | Credible product, customers, funding, brand, or serious GTM motion. |
| Application complexity | 8 | Direct ATS, Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, or manageable company page. Heavy Workday/questionnaires score lower. |

Score thresholds:

- 9.0–10: Exceptional. Route to Pipeline / escalate for review.
- 8.0–8.9: Strong. Write to Pipeline as New or Review depending on workflow.
- 7.0–7.9: Interesting but incomplete. Hold for review or include in extras; do not prioritize unless asked.
- 6.0–6.9: Plausible adjacent role; generally skip unless there is a special reason.
- Below 6.0: Ignore.

## Salary policy

Missing salary should be penalized but not treated as an automatic rejection if the role is otherwise exceptional. If written to Pipeline without salary, set salary to “Not listed” and explain the exception in fit assessment.
