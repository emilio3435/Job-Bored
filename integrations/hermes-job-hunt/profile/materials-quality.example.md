# Materials Quality Contract (template — copy to materials-quality.md)

Goal: produce tailored resumes + cover letters that are evidence-dense,
role-specific, and fit the target page count. Personalize as needed.

## Success means
- Resume renders as a clean 1 or 2 pages (decided before drafting).
- Cover letter is one page with concrete evidence.
- Only verified facts from `profile.md`, `resume-bullets.md`, `voice.md`, the job
  description, and the job-analysis notes. No invented metrics or unverified claims.
- `qa-report.md` records page count, evidence used, omissions, and caveats.

## Visual assets
- Preserve the template's `<img>` logo marks and their `onerror="this.remove()"`
  attribute so a missing logo is dropped, not shown broken. The logo resolver
  auto-fetches marks by company name/domain.

Stop when the resume, cover letter, job analysis, and QA report are complete and
the page-fit checks pass.
